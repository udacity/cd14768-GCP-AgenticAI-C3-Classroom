# Electro Volt: The Series - Episode Summaries

**Episode 1: The Spark**

Electrical engineer Marcus Jones is struck by lightning during a freak accident. He awakens with the ability to control electricity. He struggles to control his new powers, accidentally causing a city-wide blackout. The episode ends with Marcus on the run from the authorities, who believe he is a dangerous criminal.

**Episode 2: The Current**

Marcus learns to control his powers, using them to stop a runaway train and save hundreds of lives. He becomes a local hero, and is given the name Electro Volt. He is then confronted by the Iron Tyrant, a villain who can control technology. The episode ends with the Iron Tyrant challenging Electro Volt to a fight.

**Episode 3: The Iron Fist**

Electro Volt battles the Iron Tyrant, but is no match for his advanced technology. He is defeated and captured by the Iron Tyrant. The episode ends with Electro Volt being held in a high-tech prison, with no hope of escape.

**Episode 4: The Ghost in the Machine**

Electro Volt uses his technopathy to escape from the Iron Tyrant's prison. He realizes that he can't defeat the Iron Tyrant on his own, and that he needs help. He seeks out the Shadow Phantom, a vigilante who is also fighting the Iron Tyrant. The episode ends with Electro Volt and the Shadow Phantom agreeing to team up to take down the Iron Tyrant.

**Episode 5: The Shadow's Edge**

Electro Volt and the Shadow Phantom team up to take down the Iron Tyrant. They dismantle his operations and defeat his minions, but the Iron Tyrant always seems to be one step ahead of them. The episode ends with them discovering that the Iron Tyrant has a secret weapon: a device that can control all of the world's technology.

**Episode 6: The Heart of the Machine**

Electro Volt and the Shadow Phantom confront the Iron Tyrant in his throne room. They manage to defeat him, but not before he uploads his consciousness to the internet. The episode ends with the Iron Tyrant's digital ghost laughing as his physical body is destroyed.

**Episode 7: The Digital Ghost**

The Iron Tyrant's consciousness is now loose on the internet, and he can control any technology he wants. He causes chaos and destruction around the world, and the heroes are powerless to stop him. The episode ends with Electro Volt coming up with a risky plan: to enter the digital world and confront the Iron Tyrant's digital ghost on his own turf.

**Episode 8: The Firewall**

Electro Volt and the Shadow Phantom enter the digital world to confront the Iron Tyrant's digital ghost. They are in his world now, and he has complete control over everything. The episode ends with the two heroes about to be deleted by the Iron Tyrant, with no hope of escape.

**Episode 9: The Final Countdown**

The heroes are saved by a mysterious digital entity that gives them the power to fight the Iron Tyrant on equal terms. They engage in a massive digital battle, with the fate of the world hanging in the balance. The episode ends with the heroes about to deliver the final blow to the Iron Tyrant.

**Episode 10: The Ghost in the Shell**

The heroes defeat the Iron Tyrant, but not before he manages to download his consciousness into a new and more powerful robotic body. The episode ends with the heroes facing off against the new and improved Iron Tyrant, with no guarantee that they will be able to defeat him.
