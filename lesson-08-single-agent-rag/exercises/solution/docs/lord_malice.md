# Lord Malice

**Real Name:** Unknown

**Powers:**

- **Dark Energy Manipulation:** Lord Malice can control and manipulate dark energy, a powerful and destructive force. He can use it to create energy blasts, drain the life force of others, and corrupt living beings.
- **Immortality:** He is immortal and has lived for centuries, accumulating vast knowledge and power.
- **Mind Control:** He can control the minds of others, forcing them to do his bidding.

**Origin:**
Lord Malice is an ancient and powerful being from another dimension. He seeks to conquer all of reality and plunge it into eternal darkness. He is the arch-nemesis of Captain Comet.
