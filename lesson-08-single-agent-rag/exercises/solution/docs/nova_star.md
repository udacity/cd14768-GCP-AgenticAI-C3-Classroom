# Nova Star

**Real Name:** Maya Lin

**Powers:**

- **Stellar Manipulation:** Nova Star can create and control miniature stars, using them as projectiles or sources of intense heat and light.
- **Gravity Control:** She can manipulate gravitational fields, allowing her to levitate objects, create gravitational pulses, and even bend light.
- **Enhanced Agility and Reflexes:** Her connection to the stars grants her superhuman agility and reflexes.

**Origin:**
Maya Lin was an astronomer who discovered a mysterious celestial object that granted her stellar powers. She now uses her abilities to protect the innocent as Nova Star.
