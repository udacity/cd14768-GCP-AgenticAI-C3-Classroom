# Serpentina

**Real Name:** Unknown

**Powers:**

- **Serpentine Physiology:** Serpentina has the abilities of a snake. She is incredibly flexible, can dislocate her joints, and has a venomous bite.
- **Superhuman Agility and Reflexes:** She is incredibly fast and agile, able to dodge bullets and move with lightning speed.
- **Master of Poisons:** She is an expert in the creation and use of all types of poisons and toxins.

**Origin:**
Serpentina was a member of a secret society of assassins known as the Serpent's Coil. She was their most deadly operative, but was betrayed by her own order. She now works as a freelance assassin, selling her skills to the highest bidder. She is a recurring foe of Shadow Phantom.
