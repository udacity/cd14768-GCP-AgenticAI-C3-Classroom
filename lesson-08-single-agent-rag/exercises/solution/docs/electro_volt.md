# Electro Volt

**Real Name:** Marcus Jones

**Powers:**

- **Electrokinesis:** Electro Volt can create and control electricity. He can project bolts of lightning, create electromagnetic pulses, and absorb electricity to enhance his strength and speed.
- **Technopathy:** He can communicate with and control electronic devices.
- **Superhuman Speed:** He can move at superhuman speeds by transforming into a being of pure electricity.

**Origin:**
Marcus Jones was an electrical engineer who was struck by lightning during a freak accident. The accident gave him the power to control electricity, and he now uses his powers to fight crime as Electro Volt.
