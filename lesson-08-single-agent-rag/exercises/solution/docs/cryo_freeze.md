# Cryo Freeze

**Real Name:** Dr. Evelyn Reed

**Powers:**

- **Cryokinesis:** Cryo Freeze can create and control ice and cold. She can freeze objects and people, create ice constructs, and lower the temperature of her surroundings to absolute zero.
- **Cold Immunity:** She is immune to all forms of cold.
- **Ice Mimicry:** She can turn her body into solid ice, granting her enhanced strength and durability.

**Origin:**
Dr. Evelyn Reed was a cryogenics researcher who was exposed to a new and unstable cryogenic compound. The compound gave her the power to control ice and cold, and she now uses her abilities to fight crime as Cryo Freeze.
