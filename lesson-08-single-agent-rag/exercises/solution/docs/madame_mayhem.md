# Madame Mayhem

**Real Name:** Isabella Dubois

**Powers:**
- **Chaos Magic:** Madame Mayhem can manipulate the forces of chaos, causing unpredictable and destructive events to occur. She can create illusions, teleport, and alter reality on a small scale.
- **Enhanced Strength and Agility:** Her connection to chaos magic grants her superhuman strength and agility.
- **Weapon Master:** She is an expert in the use of various weapons, including swords, knives, and whips.

**Origin:**
Isabella Dubois was a sorceress who became obsessed with chaos magic. She was exiled from her order for her dangerous and unethical practices, and now seeks to spread chaos and destruction wherever she goes. She is the arch-nemesis of Nova Star.
