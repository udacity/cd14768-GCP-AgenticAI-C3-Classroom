# Iron Tyrant

**Real Name:** Marcus Thorne

**Powers:**

- **Technopathy:** The Iron Tyrant can control and manipulate all forms of technology. He can use it to create advanced robots, hack into any computer system, and control the world's infrastructure.
- **Genius-Level Intellect:** He is a brilliant inventor and strategist, always several steps ahead of his opponents.
- **Powered Armor:** He wears a suit of advanced powered armor that is constantly evolving and adapting to new threats.

**Origin:**
Marcus Thorne was a wealthy industrialist who became obsessed with technology and control. He believes that humanity is flawed and that the only way to save it is to place it under his absolute control. He is the arch-nemesis of Electro Volt.
