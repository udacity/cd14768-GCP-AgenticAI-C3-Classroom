# Chrono Shifter: The Series - Episode Summaries

**Episode 1: The Sands of Time**

Physicist Dr. Alistair Finch creates a time machine. During the machine's first test, there is an accident that gives him the power to control time. He struggles to control his new powers, accidentally trapping himself in a time loop. The episode ends with Alistair reliving the same day over and over again, with no hope of escape.

**Episode 2: The Stitch in Time**

Alistair learns to control his powers, using them to break free from the time loop. He also uses his powers to save a group of people from a train crash. He becomes a local hero, and is given the name Chrono Shifter. He is then confronted by Professor Paradox, a villain who can also manipulate time. The episode ends with Professor Paradox challenging Chrono Shifter to a duel.

**Episode 3: The Paradox**

Chrono Shifter battles Professor Paradox, but their powers are evenly matched. The episode ends with them creating a massive temporal paradox that threatens to unravel all of reality.

**Episode 4: The Nick of Time**

Chrono Shifter uses his temporal awareness to find a way to undo the paradox. He succeeds, but not before Professor Paradox escapes. The episode ends with Chrono Shifter realizing that he has a responsibility to use his powers to protect the timeline.

**Episode 5: The Sands of a Different Hourglass**

Chrono Shifter learns that Professor Paradox is from the future, and that he has come to the past to prevent a terrible catastrophe. He must decide whether to trust the Professor, or to fight him and risk destroying the world. The episode ends with Chrono Shifter agreeing to help Professor Paradox, but only on his own terms.

**Episode 6: The Future's Past**

Chrono Shifter and Professor Paradox team up with Cryo Freeze and Pyro Clast to prevent the catastrophe. They learn that the catastrophe is a new ice age caused by Cryo Freeze and Pyro Clast's powers growing unstable. The episode ends with the heroes about to activate a device that will stabilize their powers, but it is incredibly risky.

**Episode 7: The Eleventh Hour**

The heroes activate the device, but it has an unforeseen side effect: it creates a portal to another dimension. A powerful and ancient being known as the Void Reaver emerges from the portal and threatens to consume all of reality. The episode ends with the heroes about to be consumed by the Void Reaver.

**Episode 8: The Moment of Truth**

The heroes are saved by the timely intervention of the Wardens, a secret organization that monitors super-powered individuals. The Wardens provide the heroes with advanced technology that allows them to fight the Void Reaver on equal terms. The episode ends with the heroes ready to confront the Void Reaver again, this time for the fate of all of reality.

**Episode 9: The End of Time**

The heroes confront the Void Reaver in its own dimension. They are much more powerful than before, and manage to hold their own against it. But the Void Reaver reveals that it has a trump card: it can erase people from existence, making it so that they never even existed. The episode ends with the Void Reaver about to erase Chrono Shifter from existence.

**Episode 10: A Stitch in Time Saves Nine**

Chrono Shifter uses his powers to create a temporal paradox, saving himself from being erased from existence. He then uses his powers to trap the Void Reaver in a time loop, giving the other heroes the chance to defeat it. The episode ends with the heroes victorious, but they know that it is only a matter of time before the Void Reaver escapes from its prison.
