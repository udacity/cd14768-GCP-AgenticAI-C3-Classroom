# Aqua Marine

**Real Name:** Marina Cortez

**Powers:**

- **Hydrokinesis:** Aqua Marine can control and manipulate water in all its forms (liquid, ice, steam). She can create powerful water currents, tidal waves, and ice constructs.
- **Aquatic Respiration:** She can breathe underwater and withstand the pressures of the deep ocean.
- **Marine Life Communication:** She can communicate with and command all forms of marine life.

**Origin:**
Marina Cortez was a marine biologist who was bonded with an ancient oceanic artifact that granted her control over water. She now protects the oceans and the world's waterways as Aqua Marine.
