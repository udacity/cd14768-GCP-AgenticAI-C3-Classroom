# Captain Comet

**Real Name:** Alex Ryder

**Powers:**

- **Cosmic Energy Manipulation:** Captain Comet can absorb and manipulate cosmic energy, allowing him to fly, create energy blasts, and generate force fields.
- **Superhuman Strength and Durability:** His body is enhanced by cosmic energy, granting him superhuman strength and resistance to injury.
- **Teleportation:** He can teleport across vast distances by folding space-time.

**Origin:**
Alex Ryder was an astrophysicist who was exposed to a rare cosmic radiation during a deep-space mission. The radiation unlocked his latent superhuman abilities, and he became the hero known as Captain Comet. He now protects the Earth from cosmic threats.
