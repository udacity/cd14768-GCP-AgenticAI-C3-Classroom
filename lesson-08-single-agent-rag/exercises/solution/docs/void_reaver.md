# Void Reaver

**Real Name:** Unknown

**Powers:**

- **Void Manipulation:** The Void Reaver can control and manipulate the void, a dimension of pure nothingness. He can use it to erase objects and people from existence, create portals, and trap his enemies in a pocket dimension.
- **Intangibility:** He can become intangible, allowing him to pass through solid objects.
- **Energy Absorption:** He can absorb all forms of energy, making him incredibly difficult to harm.

**Origin:**
The Void Reaver is a being from the void itself, a creature of pure entropy and destruction. It seeks to consume all of reality and return it to the nothingness from whence it came. It is a threat to all of existence.
