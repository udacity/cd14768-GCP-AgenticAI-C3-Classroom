# Shadow Phantom

**Real Name:** Kenji Tanaka

**Powers:**

- **Shadow Manipulation:** Shadow Phantom can control and solidify shadows, creating weapons, shields, and constructs. He can also become intangible by merging with shadows.
- **Invisibility:** He can become completely invisible in low-light conditions.
- **Enhanced Stealth:** He is a master of stealth and espionage.

**Origin:**
Kenji Tanaka was a ninja who was trained in the art of shadow manipulation. After his clan was wiped out by a mysterious organization, he became the Shadow Phantom, a vigilante who uses his powers to fight injustice from the shadows.
