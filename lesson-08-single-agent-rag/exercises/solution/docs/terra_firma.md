# Terra Firma

**Real Name:** Dr. Elias Thorne

**Powers:**

- **Geokinesis:** Terra Firma can control and manipulate earth and rock, creating earthquakes, fissures, and rock slides. He can also shape rock into weapons and shields.
- **Superhuman Strength and Durability:** His connection to the earth grants him immense strength and durability.
- **Earth Empathy:** He can sense vibrations in the earth, allowing him to detect movement and potential threats.

**Origin:**
Dr. Elias Thorne was a geologist who was exposed to a strange geological phenomenon that gave him the power to control the earth. He now uses his powers to protect the planet from those who would harm it.
