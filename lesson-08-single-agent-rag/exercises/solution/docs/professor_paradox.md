# Professor Paradox

**Real Name:** Dr. Phineas Tempus

**Powers:**

- **Time Manipulation:** Professor Paradox can create temporal paradoxes, causing time to loop, stutter, and even reverse. He can also summon beings and objects from different time periods.
- **Genius-Level Intellect:** He is a brilliant historian and temporal physicist.
- **Temporal Immunity:** He is immune to the effects of his own temporal paradoxes.

**Origin:**
Dr. Phineas Tempus was a historian who became obsessed with correcting what he saw as the mistakes of the past. He created a device that allowed him to manipulate time, but it also fractured his mind, making him believe that he could create a perfect timeline by any means necessary. He is the arch-nemesis of Chrono Shifter.
