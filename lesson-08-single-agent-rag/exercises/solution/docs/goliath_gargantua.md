# Goliath Gargantua

**Real Name:** Dr. Gideon Graves

**Powers:**

- **Size Manipulation:** Goliath Gargantua can grow to immense sizes, granting him superhuman strength and durability. He can also shrink to the size of an insect.
- **Genius-Level Intellect:** He is a brilliant scientist with expertise in biology and genetics.
- **Superhuman Durability:** Even at his normal size, his body is incredibly dense and resistant to injury.

**Origin:**
Dr. Gideon Graves was a scientist who was obsessed with size and strength. He created a formula that allowed him to grow to incredible sizes, but it also warped his mind, making him arrogant and power-hungry. He now seeks to prove that he is the strongest being on Earth. He is a recurring foe of Terra Firma.
