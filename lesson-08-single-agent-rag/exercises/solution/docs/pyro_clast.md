# Pyro Clast

**Real Name:** Dante Cruz

**Powers:**

- **Pyrokinesis:** Pyro Clast can create and control fire and heat. He can project fire from his body, create fireballs, and raise the temperature of his surroundings to extreme levels.
- **Magma Control:** He can control and manipulate molten rock, creating volcanic eruptions and lava flows.
- **Heat Immunity:** He is immune to all forms of heat and fire.

**Origin:**
Dante Cruz was a volcanologist who fell into a volcano and was presumed dead. He emerged with the power to control fire and magma, and now fights for justice as Pyro Clast.
