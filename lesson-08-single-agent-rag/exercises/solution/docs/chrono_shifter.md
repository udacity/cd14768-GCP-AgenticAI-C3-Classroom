# Chrono Shifter

**Real Name:** Dr. Alistair Finch

**Powers:**

- **Chronokinesis:** Chrono Shifter can manipulate time. He can slow down, speed up, and even stop time. He can also travel through time, although this is very draining on his powers.
- **Temporal Awareness:** He can see into the past and future, and can perceive multiple timelines at once.
- **Age Manipulation:** He can manipulate the age of objects and living beings.

**Origin:**
Dr. Alistair Finch was a physicist who created a time machine. During the machine's first test, there was an accident that gave him the power to control time. He now uses his powers to protect the timeline from those who would try to alter it for their own selfish purposes.
