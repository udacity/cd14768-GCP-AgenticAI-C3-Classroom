# Echo Chamber

**Real Name:** Unknown

**Powers:**

- **Sound Absorption and Redirection:** Echo Chamber can absorb all sound in his vicinity and redirect it as a powerful sonic blast. He can also create areas of complete silence.
- **Voice Mimicry:** He can perfectly mimic any voice he has ever heard.
- **Enhanced Hearing:** He has superhuman hearing, able to hear a pin drop from miles away.

**Origin:**
Echo Chamber was a sound engineer who was exposed to a strange acoustic energy that gave him control over sound. He now uses his powers to commit crimes and create chaos. He is a recurring foe of Aero Dynamo.
