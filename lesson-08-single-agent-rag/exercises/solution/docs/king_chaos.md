# King Chaos

**Real Name:** Unknown

**Powers:**

- **Chaos Embodiment:** King Chaos is the living embodiment of chaos. He can warp reality, create and destroy matter, and manipulate the laws of physics. His power is unpredictable and difficult to control, even for himself.
- **Immortality:** He is an immortal being who has existed since the beginning of time.
- **Nigh-Omniscience:** He has a vast and cosmic awareness, but his perception of reality is fractured and chaotic.

**Origin:**
King Chaos is a primordial being who seeks to undo all of creation and return the universe to a state of pure chaos. He is a cosmic-level threat who has fought against many of the world's greatest heroes.
